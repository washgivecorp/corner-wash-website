Your Friendly Local Laundromat

Corner Wash is easily located on the corner of Adams Ave. and 35th St. You can find our self service laundry across the street from the Adams Recreation Center. We have plenty of parking in our lot behind the Laundromat and on the street. And if you get hungry, we are a short walk away from Dos Palmas Cafe, Subway and Brothers Mexican Restaurant.

Come by any time between 7:00 am – 7:30 pm (the time of last wash) to do your own laundry, or drop off your laundry any time before 6:00 pm to utilize our convenient wash and fold service. At Corner Wash everyone is welcome!
